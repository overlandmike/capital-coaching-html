Capital Coaching Ltd was established in 2006 and is one of Wellington’s largest and most prestigious Tennis Coaching Academies. We operate out of four premier facilities in the Wellington and Hutt Regions and we also work with numerous primary and secondary schools. Capital Coaching believes in collaboration with local and international coaches and academies to ensure that the most accurate and up to date coaching advice is being provided and that international pathways are attainable for players.

Capital Coaching currently works with Tennis New Zealand and Tennis Australia representatives.
