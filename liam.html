Meet our

Coaches

Liam

Coach

Liam is a university student, has been at Karori United as assistant coach for nearly six years. Liam started playing at the Fendalton and Elmwood Clubs in Christchurch, where he was trained by well- known coaches Alistair Hunt and Robyn Hunt and was a keen interclub player through his junior years. He began coaching with Capital Coaching in 2014. He has completed the 40-hour Community Coaches Course run by Tennis NZ and is in the process of completing the 120-hour Junior Development Course, an internationally recognised coaching qualification which he is expects to finish this year. Liam focuses on developing players in junior and intermediate academies.
