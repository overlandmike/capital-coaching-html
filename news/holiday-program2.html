Holiday Program

September / October 2019

Our September / October School Holiday program will be running for both weeks of the holiday. This is a great way to get ready for junior interclub which starts straight after the holidays.
The program will occur no matter what the weather, and we have at least two coaches on every day to ensure that we cater for different abilities.
Students are welcome to stay all day (but bring a packed lunch) or for half days either mornings or afternoons. The holiday program starts at 9am, but our coaches will be at the clubrooms by 8.30am.


Week 1 - (5 days) Monday 30th September – Friday 4th October.

  • Foundation Coaching for 6 -12 year olds, who don't yet play interclub (no previous tennis experience required) 9am - 12.30pm: $120
  • Competitor Coaching for 8 -14 year olds: Interclub and Tournament Players (players younger than 8 will be considered on ability) 1.30 - 4.30pm: $120
  • All Day Coaching Programme targets all players, and is designed for the working parent. Drop off from 8.30am, with pick up by 4.30pm. $240

Week 2 – (5 days) Monday 7th October - Friday 11th October.

  • Foundation Coaching for 6 -12 year olds, who don't yet play interclub (no previous tennis experience required) 9am-12.30pm: $120
  • Competitor Coaching for 8 -14 year olds: Interclub and Tournament Players (players younger than 8 will be considered on ability) 1.30 - 4.30pm: $120
  • All Day Coaching Programme targets all players. Drop off from 8.30am, with pick up by 4.30pm. $240.

Partial Weeks

Preference will be given to full week enrollments. However, if you wish to enroll prior to the start of the programme for a few days we will accommodate this if space allows. $45 per half day session, $80 for a full day.
To register for the holiday program please email info@capitalcoaching.kiwi or complete the registration form.
