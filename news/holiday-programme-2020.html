Holiday Program

January 2020

Have you been watching the ASB Open and getting enthusiastic about tennis.

Our January School Holiday program will now be running for the next three weeks of the holiday. We were going to be running this from Wellington Anniversary Day onwards but due to demand we are holding a morning program in the week Monday 13th January – Friday 17th January, and then full days from 21st January 2020 onwards.

The program will occur no matter what the weather, and we ensure that we cater for different abilities.
Students are welcome to stay all day (from the second week onwards - but bring a packed lunch) or for half days either mornings or afternoons. The holiday program starts at 9am, but our coaches will be at the clubrooms by 8.30am.

PLEASE NOTE – BOOKINGS ARE ESSENTIAL – email info@capitalcoaching.kiwi


Week 1 - (5 days) Monday 13th January – Friday 17th January.

  • Foundation Coaching for 6 -12 year olds, who don't yet play interclub (no previous tennis experience required) 9am - 12.30pm: $120

Week 2 – (4 days) Tuesday 21st January - Friday 24th January.

  • Foundation Coaching for 6 -12 year olds, who don't yet play interclub (no previous tennis experience required) 9am-12.30pm: $120
  • Competitor Coaching for 8 -14 year olds: Interclub and Tournament Players (players younger than 8 will be considered on ability) 1.30 - 4.30pm: $120
  • All Day Coaching Programme targets all players. Drop off from 8.30am, with pick up by 4.30pm. $240.

Week 3 – (4 days) Monday 27th January - Friday 31st January.

  • Foundation Coaching for 6 -12 year olds, who don't yet play interclub (no previous tennis experience required) 9am-12.30pm: $120
  • Competitor Coaching for 8 -14 year olds: Interclub and Tournament Players (players younger than 8 will be considered on ability) 1.30 - 4.30pm: $120
  • All Day Coaching Programme targets all players. Drop off from 8.30am, with pick up by 4.30pm. $240.

Partial Weeks

Preference will be given to full week enrolments. However, if you wish to enrol prior to the start of the programme for a few days we will accommodate this if space allows. $45 per half day session, $80 for a full day.


To register for the holiday program please email info@capitalcoaching.kiwi
