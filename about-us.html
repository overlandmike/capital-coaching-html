Who we are

About Us

Wellingtons Largest and most prestigious Tennis Coaching Academy

We operate out of four premier facilities in the Wellington and Hutt Regions and we also work with numerous primary and secondary schools. Andy Wheeler and his team of professional and internationally experienced coaches support players to develop their ultimate potential both on and off-court. We specialise in coaching for all ages and abilities, with pathways that support our youngest peewees right through to national and international players in our high performance pathways. Our track record includes being awarded the prestigious New Zealand Tennis Club Coach of the YearAward in 2012 and numerous National Event tittles. Many of our juniors play international ITFs and regularly compete overseas at International Junior Competitions. Capital Coaching and Consulting, provides clients with technical advice, strengthens and improves match play, game technique and sport psyche to ensure success in tennis. Capital Coaching understands the value of providing consistent and credible coaching advice, and therefore ensures that its team of coaches are fully trained with either New Zealand or European coaching qualifications and several have had extensive international coaching and playing experience.
