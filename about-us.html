About Us

Capital Coaching offers coaching for 11 months of each year. We pride ourselves in having some of the most qualified tennis coaches in Wellington. Our coaches include coaches with international experience who are able to cater for all ages, stages, skills and ambitions. Approximately 60 % of our students do tennis year around, with the remainder doing tennis during terms 4 and term 1.

We are Karori focused. We work closely with the committee and officials of Karori United Tennis Club, providing coach input into Interclub team composition based upon our knowledge of the juniors doing coaching, and can advise and provide pathways to start competing in local, regional and national championships and tournaments.

We are focused on ensuring that juniors build skills to grow as good players, who enjoy and play competitive tennis. For those juniors who are ambitious and possess the necessary work ethic, we have the coaches to grow them into great players.
