Who we are

About Us

Wellingtons Largest and most prestigious Tennis Coaching Academy

Capital Coaching Ltd was established in 2006 and is one of Wellington’s largest and most prestigious Tennis Coaching Academies. We have held the coaching contract for Karori United Tennis Club since 2014. Karori United is one of the largest tennis clubs in the Tennis Central Region the Tennis Central tennis club of the year, 2016. We provide highly qualified coaches for novices, beginners, emerging and advanced juniors, and also provide adult coaching and cardio tennis for adults. All our coaches have achieved, or are completing, internationally recognised coaching qualifications and the majority of our coaches have or currently play Premier Division 1 Interclub competitions, the highest Tennis Central local competitions.

We aim to make tennis fun for all. The majority of our players are juniors, and ensuring they enjoy and want to advance their tennis is a critical aspect of their development. We support their progress and develop their skills to help them be competitive in the grades they want to compete in. We have coached many juniors who have played at regional and national levels. Our coaches have skills that can be adapted to all players – including novices. Our juniors are aged 6-17 years, but we also provide coaching, through cardio tennis, to many in their 60s and 70s. After all …. tennis is a game for life.
